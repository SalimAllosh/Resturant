# Resturant App

Mobile application designed to provide a seamless and convenient food ordering experience for users. It combines a variety of features to offer an extensive range of functionalities, from user authentication and personalized recommendations to order management and meal exploration.

Key Features:

1.  Sign Up / Sign In :
    
    -   The Sign Up feature allows new users to create an account within the app. Users provide their basic information such as name, email, and password to register. The feature ensures data validation and may include additional steps like email verification. Upon successful sign-up, users can proceed to access the app's functionalities.
    
    -   The Sign In feature enables registered users to log into the app using their credentials. Users enter their email and password to authenticate their identity. The feature includes robust security measures like encryption and authentication checks to protect user accounts. Once authenticated, users gain access to personalized content and features.

2.  Recommended Meals:
    
    -   The Recommended Meals feature provides users with personalized meal recommendations based on their preferences, dietary restrictions, and previous orders.  Users can browse through the recommended meals, view details such as ingredients and nutritional information, and add them to their shopping cart or place an order.

3.  Popular Food:
    
    -   The Popular Food feature showcases a list of trending or popular food items within the app. The list is dynamically updated based on factors like customer ratings, order frequency, or current trends. Users can explore popular food options, view details about each item, and add them to their cart or place an order.

4.  Meal Detail:
    
    -   The Meal Detail feature provides users with comprehensive information about a specific meal. Users can access details such as meal name, description, ingredients, nutritional information, customer ratings, and reviews. The feature may also include additional features like sharing meal details, saving favorites, or adding the meal to the cart for easy ordering.

5.  Orders:
    
    -   The Orders feature allows users to place and track their orders within the app. Users can select meals from the menu, customize their order, and proceed to the checkout process.  Users can track the status of their orders, receive notifications about order updates, and view order history.

6.  Shopping Cart:
    
    -   The Shopping Cart feature allows users to add meals to their cart as they browse through the app. Users can review and modify their cart contents, apply promotional codes or discounts, and proceed to checkout to place the final order. The feature ensures a smooth and intuitive cart management experience for users.

7.  Profile :
    
    -   The Profile feature enables users to manage their profile information and includes an integration with location services. Users can update their personal details, such as name, profile picture.
    -  Additionally, users can provide their location information to receive more accurate recommendations, local offers, or delivery services.

The app utilizes the GetX state management library to handle state management throughout the application.
